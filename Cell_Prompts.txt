COMIC STRIP IMAGE-GENERATION PROMPTS
"Bea Burps" — Cells 2, 3, 6, 7, 8, 9
=====================================

These prompts are written to match the established art style and
characters from the already-completed panels (cell_1_pre_arm_edit.png,
cell_4.png, cell_5.png) and the reference portraits in /portraits.

GLOBAL STYLE GUIDE (include in every prompt)
---------------------------------------------
- Style: clean digital cartoon/comic illustration, bold black outlines,
  warm flat-cel shading, slightly stylized proportions (same style as
  cell_1_pre_arm_edit.png, cell_4.png, and cell_5.png).
- Panel format: square comic panel with a small white numbered box in
  the top-left corner containing the cell number (e.g. "2", "3", "6", etc.),
  matching the numbering style seen in cells 1, 4, and 5.
- All left/right directions are from the AUDIENCE's point of view.
- Sound effects / exclamations should be rendered as bold, colorful
  comic-style lettering with an outline, matching the "CHOMP! GULP!
  CCRRUNCH!" and "SLURP! SLURP! SLURP! *GLUG-GLUG*" treatments in
  cells 4 and 5.

CHARACTER REFERENCE SHEET
---------------------------------------------
- Moxie: young woman, shoulder-length straight orange/red hair with side
  part, green eyes, light freckles/blush, wearing a maroon/burgundy
  short-sleeve t-shirt and light blue jeans (see
  portraits/Moxie_CharacterPortrait.png).
- Keith: young man, short tousled blond hair, blue eyes, wearing a
  green short-sleeve t-shirt and tan/khaki pants, brown shoes (see
  cell_1_pre_arm_edit.png).
- Bea: small white West Highland Terrier (Westie) mix with a fluffy
  coat, dark button eyes, pink harness, perky triangular ears, and a
  fluffy upright tail (see portraits/Bea_Portrait.jpg and
  portraits/Bea_RearPortrait.jpg).

SETTING REFERENCE
---------------------------------------------
- Living room: dark brown leather couch, warm amber lamp light, potted
  plants, a TV on a low stand to the left, a side table with a framed
  photo (see cell_1_pre_arm_edit.png).
- Kitchen: light wood cabinets, white tile floor, a dishwasher, food and
  water bowls on a textured mat (see cell_4.png and cell_5.png).
- Hallway/transition space connects the living room (audience-left) to
  the kitchen (audience-right).
